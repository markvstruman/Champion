Achalasia

What is it?

Achalasia is a rare disorder, making it
difficult for food and liquid to pass
into the stomach.

Fact: More than 80 diseases occur
because of the immune system attacking
the body's own organs, tissues, and
cells.

What are the Symptoms?

Achalasia patients may experience one
or more of the following:

  • Back-up of food
  • Regurgitation
  • Chest pain
  • Weight loss
